## Cultural Significance

The oil lamp (called "Pahana" in Sinhala or "Deepam" in Tamil) holds deep spiritual significance in Sri Lankan and Indian cultures:

- **Symbol of Knowledge**: The flame represents the light of wisdom dispelling darkness of ignorance
- **Auspicious Ritual**: Lighting oil lamps is performed during religious ceremonies, festivals, and special occasions
- **Offering**: It's considered an offering to deities and brings blessings and positive energy
- **Bodhi Tree**: The sacred fig tree under which Buddha attained enlightenment, represented in the background
